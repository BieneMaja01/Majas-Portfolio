Hello, my name is Maja Grandic, I am 24 years old and currently in my sixth semester studying Communication Design at the ABK Stuttgart (State Academy of Fine Arts Stuttgart). My creative focus lies in the fields of illustration, poster design, and animation. I work confidently with the Adobe Creative Suite especially Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, After Effects, Animate, and Premiere. I also have a basic working knowledge of various other design and animation programs. Through several freelance projects, I have gained valuable practical experience. For the Center for School Quality and Teacher Training (ZSL), I developed a series of animated educational clips featuring the character "Pippo the Clown." I have also created illustrative work for other clients, such as the "KBA climbing gym" in Dudweiler.
